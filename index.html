Who We Are

At Vantage Intelligence, we are a student-led innovation company focused on creating smarter solutions for modern everyday challenges. We believe technology should not distract people from success—it should help them achieve it. Our mission is to combine intelligent design, practical thinking, and modern AI tools to create products that improve productivity, focus, and daily life. Founded by a passionate team of young entrepreneurs through the Junior Achievement My Company Programme, Vantage Intelligence represents creativity, ambition, and forward-thinking leadership. Our first concept, the AI Smart Study Desk, is designed to help students learn in a focused environment by reducing phone distractions while still giving access to educational tools, AI assistance, and smart workspace features. FocusOne AI Study Desk.

FocusOne is an AI-powered smart study desk designed to help students focus, learn smarter, and reduce phone distractions. It features a built-in screen with access to Vantage AI and research tools, integrated LED lighting, and a clean productivity-focused workspace. By combining technology with a distraction-free environment, FocusOne transforms studying into a smarter and more efficient experience.

Target Audience

FocusOne is designed for students at all levels who want to improve their study habits and reduce distractions. Whether you're a high school student preparing for exams or a college student working on research projects, FocusOne provides the tools and environment you need to stay focused and productive.
